NuclearFreeSoftware

Free/Open Source Software for applications in nuclear disarmament and nuclear safety

View on GitHub

What is this?

For a variety of applications related to nuclear disarmament, arms control and non-proliferation, software is used, e.g. for verificaiton purposes. While free/open source software covers many other fields of use, it is rarely employed in the former. Increased use would be benefical. Besides ordinary advantages of Free/Open Source Software, this could increase trust among states and participation in important issues of international relations.
